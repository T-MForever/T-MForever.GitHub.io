We met in August of 2017 - Tyler's freshman and Madelein's senior years at NAU.
At the time Tyler was joining the Triathlon Team, of which Madelein was the President.

Over the course of a few weeks we became friends and got to know each other better.
We had our first date in Las Vegas during the "Team Night Out" of the Triathlon Team's annual
training camp at Lake Mead, about 30 minutes south of the Strip.



After that year we continued to date, although it was long distance, we made it work - because
somethings are just meant to last.

Throughout that period of time we got closer, began to love one another and became eachother's best
friends. After Tyler graduated and moved back home we knew we could really begin to start planning a
future together. On July 10th, 2022 we got engaged on our favorite beach in our favorite town - Laguna Beach.
