It all started in the mountains...


We met in August of 2017, at the first run workout of the year for the NAU Traithlon Team.
Our next interaction was at the first team swim practice, where although Tyler could swim,
we quickly found out he did not swim well. Madelein would spend time teaching him
how and at times laughing at his stupid jokes (or maybe she was just laughing at his form).

Fast forward a month or so, and it was time for the team's first training camp in Lake Mead, Nevada.
As fate would have it we ended up in the same car for the trip, and over the next four hours we would talk
about everything under the sun - we had so much in common that it felt like we had been best friends our whole
lives... to this day niether of us can remember a silent moment that entire trip.
Team tradition was to head to the Vegas Strip on the last night of the camp for some fun, however Tyler had managed
to convince Madelein to go to dinner with him. And so it began... our first date, October 1st 2017.


Since then we have grown together, supporting one another through all of our various schoolings, being there
for eachother through the ups and downs of being a young adult, and have only become closer, even better friends,
and falling more in love with eachother each and every day.

As we end our chapter that started in the mountains,
we start our new chapter at the beach, surrounded by the people we
love most.
